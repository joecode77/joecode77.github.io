GET TO KNOW US

What We Do

Enactus Covenant University is a non-for-profit, student-led organization that exists within Covenant University. We work to transform the lives of people in need through the power of business. We do this by setting up social enterprises that provide employment and their by-product does both social and environmental good.

Student Community

Enactus Covenant University runs social enterprises with a team of over 100 students at the forefront of social entrepreneurship; laying the path of change.

Nature of work

We work by identifying problems and carrying out needs assessments to come up with solutions to real world problems.

Partnership

We have strong partnership with the communities we work in, as well as in the corporate world to aid our delivery.

Relationship With University

We work closely with Covenant University, in order to harness their diverse expertise to help further our social enterprises' success.

Our Mission

We believe in harnessing the power of business to create sustainable and scalable change to a diverse range of beneficiary groups around the world.
Our student-led teams continually experiment with and innovative new business models to truly transform the lives of others.
Therefore our mission is to:
'Create business. Transform lives.'

Our Projects

PET CITY

Pet city is a social enterprise that focuses on reducing plastic waste (PET bottles) by converting plastic to PET bricks, while simultaneously building a waste management culture in our community. It is an enterprise owned by Enactus Covenant university in advocacy for a greener environment.

ECO-BAG

ECO-BAG is a social enterprise that creates energy retention bags aimed at solving the problem of inconsistent power supply encountered by businesses and individuals in Nigeria. ECO-BAG goes beyond energy conservation, the project seeks to empower disadvantaged groups that require financial support towards satisfying the basic human needs. ECO-BAG is borne out a commitment to sustainability. Actively participating in our communities and integrating sustainable business practices to provide decent work and tackle environmental menaces.

TOLES

Toles is a social enterprise that focuses on reducing tire waste by converting tire to shoe, while simultaneously building a waste management culture in our community. It is an enterprise owned by Enactus Covenant university in advocacy for a greener environment.
