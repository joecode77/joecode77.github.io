GET TO KNOW US

What We Do

Enactus Covenant University is a non-for-profit, student-led organization that exists within Covenant University. We work to transform the lives of people in need through the power of business. We do this by setting up social enterprises that provide employment and their by-product does both social and environmental good.

Student Community

Enactus Covenant University runs social enterprises with a team of over 100 students at the forefront of social entrepreneurship; laying the path of change.

Nature of work

We work by identifying problems and carrying out needs assessments to come up with solutions to real world problems.

Partnership

We have strong partnership with the communities we work in, as well as in the corporate world to aid our delivery.

Relationship With University

We work closely with Covenant University, in order to harness their diverse expertise to help further our social enterprises' success.

Our Mission

We believe in harnessing the power of business to create sustainable and scalable change to a diverse range of beneficiary groups around the world.
Our student-led teams continually experiment with and innovative new business models to truly transform the lives of others.
Therefore our mission is to:
'Create business. Transform lives.'

UN Sustainable Development Goals

In 2015, world leaders agreed to 17 Global Goals for sustainable development. These goals have the power to create a better world by 2030, by ending poverty, fighting inequality and addressing the urgency of climate change. Guided by the goals, it is now up to all of us. At Enactus, we've found an approach that works. One that creates sustainable, lasting change. Entrepreneurial action and business innovation that creates social impact.

Together, we can achieve the Global Goals.
